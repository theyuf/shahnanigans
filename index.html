Who are we?

Shahnanigans is a boutique creative studio based in Belfast, Northern Ireland.

We develop creative and innovative marketing solutions through digital design and augmented reality. 

What? Why? How?

It is becoming more and more difficult for brands and organisations to market their brands on social media as attention spans of online audiences are going down. Traditional digital ads get lost in clutter online and people seem to scroll past them.

Augmented reality Instagram and Facebook filters have immense potential as marketing tools for brands. Through filters, brands can not only engage with current audiences but users of these AR filters can exponentially increase exposure for your brand.
