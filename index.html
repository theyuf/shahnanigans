How do you connect with your audience? Is there a way to interact with people on social media in a fun new way? 

It is becoming more difficult to market your brands on social media as the attention span of online audiences is going down. Traditional digital ads get lost in the clutter online and people seem to scroll past them. Augmented reality (AR) filters change that.

How do AR filters change that?

Augmented reality filters allow you to interact with your audience and build loyal communities. It is easier to promote and communicate with your audience using filters as the audience gets to participate in your campaign. It brings about a sense of collectivism through an individual experience. And numbers don’t lie!

A filter that is developed and executed creatively has the potential to go viral leading to a higher number of impressions and a lower cost per user. Your users engage with your brand leading to more exposure and traffic when they create content using your filters.
